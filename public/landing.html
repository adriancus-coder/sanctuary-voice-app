Live translation for the whole room, without losing the service.

Sanctuary Voice helps churches offer real-time AI translation on participant phones, main screens, and operator controls from one simple service desk.

Everything in one panel.

The same admin desk runs live transcription, translation, songs, the main screen, operator roles, and the participant view — without juggling apps.

Live transcription

Speech from the operator mic flows through OpenAI Whisper or Azure Speech and lands on phones in a few seconds.

AI translation

Every line is rendered into each target language; refrains and repeated phrases come back instantly from cache.

Single & dual main screen

Project one language full-width or two side by side, with size presets that read clearly across the sanctuary.

Song workflow

Save lyrics in a shared church library, jump between verses and choruses, repeat the refrain with a single R: line.

Pinned text & black screen

One click to show a fixed announcement, a pinned Bible verse, or an instant black screen — without breaking participant translations.

Operator roles & access requests

Sign in with a PIN or request access from the front page; the admin grants Full, Main Screen only, or Song only and you drop onto the live event.

Permanent participant QR

Print one QR for the whole year. People scan it once and pick the live service from a clean chooser.

Scheduled services & countdown

Set date, time and timezone when you create a service. Visitors and the projector show a live countdown until you go on the air.

Glossary & source corrections

Lock in how Bible names, places and church-specific terms are translated — per language and per event.

Reliability & privacy

Built-in self-test covers database, sockets, OpenAI, Azure, web push and transcribe latency. Daily backups, graceful shutdown, and codes hidden by default with an eye toggle.
